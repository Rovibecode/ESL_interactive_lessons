ESL Lesson Plan: Hobbies and Free Time Activities

Discovering Passions and Sharing Interests

Target: Kids & Adults Level: A1-B1 (CEFR) Time: 60-75 Mins Focus: Frequency Adverbs & Likes/Dislikes

I. Lesson Objectives

By the end of this lesson, students will be able to:

  • 🎨 Identify and name a variety of common hobbies and free time activities.
  • ⏰ Accurately use frequency adverbs (e.g., always, usually, often, sometimes, rarely, never) to describe how frequently they or others engage in hobbies.
  • 👍👎 Express their likes, dislikes, and preferences for different activities, and compare/contrast their own hobbies with those of others.
  • 💬 Engage in simple conversations about hobbies and free time, asking and answering questions about activities, frequency, and preferences.

II. Materials

  • Whiteboard or projector
  • Markers/pens
  • Worksheet for Activity 2 and Homework (to be provided by teacher)

III. Lesson Procedure

A. Warm-up: "What Do You Do for Fun?" (10-15 minutes)

Students will share their leisure activities to introduce basic hobby vocabulary.

Introduction:

"Hello everyone! Today, we're going to talk about something fun: 'Hobbies and Free Time Activities'!"

大家好！今天我們要聊聊一些有趣的事情：「嗜好和休閒活動」！

(Dàjiā hǎo! Jīntiān wǒmen yào liáoliáo yīxiē yǒuqù de shìqíng: “shìhào hé xiūxián huódòng”!)

Key Vocabulary:

Hobbies and Free Time Activities: 嗜好和休閒活動 (shìhào hé xiūxián huódòng)
Read books: 看書 (kàn shū)
Play sports: 運動 (yùndòng)
Listen to music: 聽音樂 (tīng yīnyuè)
Watch movies: 看電影 (kàn diànyǐng)

B. Main Activities (40-45 minutes)

Two interactive scenarios to practice frequency adverbs and expressions of likes/dislikes.

Activity 1: "How Often Do You...?"

Groups ask each other about hobby frequency using adverbs, then share findings.

Review & Introduce Frequency Adverbs:

Adverb Chinese Pinyin
Always總是(zǒngshì)
Usually通常(tōngcháng)
Often經常(jīngcháng)
Sometimes有時候(yǒu shíhòu)
Rarely很少(hěn shǎo)
Never從不(cóng bù)

Examples:

"How often do you go swimming?" (你多久去游泳一次？) (Nǐ duōjiǔ qù yóuyǒng yīcì?)

"I often go swimming on weekends." (我週末經常去游泳。) (Wǒ zhōumò jīngcháng qù yóuyǒng.)

"She sometimes reads comics." (她有時候看漫畫。) (Tā yǒu shíhòu kàn mànhuà.)

Sample Hobbies:

Hiking, Swimming, Cooking, Playing Chess, Watching TV, Learning a new language, Gardening, Singing, Painting, Cycling, Volunteering.

Activity 2: "Likes, Dislikes & Preferences"

Students use various expressions to describe their feelings about hobbies and compare activities.

Expressions:

  • I love... (我愛/非常喜歡...) (wǒ ài/fēicháng xǐhuān...)
  • I really like... (我真的很喜歡...) (wǒ zhēn de hěn xǐhuān...)
  • I enjoy... (我享受...) (wǒ xiǎngshòu...)
  • I don't mind... (我不介意...) (wǒ bù jièyì...)
  • I don't like... (我不喜歡...) (wǒ bù xǐhuān...)
  • I hate... (我討厭...) (wǒ tǎoyàn...)
  • I prefer... to... (我比較喜歡...而不是...) (wǒ bǐjiào xǐhuān...ér bù shì...)

Scenario Examples:

"Your friend loves singing karaoke, but you find it boring. What do you say?"

"You have two free hours: go shopping or go hiking? Which do you prefer and why?"

"Your sister always watches TV, but your brother never plays video games. What do they like to do instead?"

C. Wrap-up: "My Hobby Profile" (5-10 minutes)

Each student summarizes their favorite hobby, frequency, and likes in 1-2 sentences.

Examples:

"I sometimes go hiking because I love being in nature." (我偶爾去爬山，因為我喜歡親近大自然。) (Wǒ ǒu'ěr qù páshān, yīnwèi wǒ xǐhuān qīnjìn dà zìrán.)

"She usually plays piano, and she enjoys practicing new songs." (她通常彈鋼琴，她喜歡練習新歌。) (Tā tōngcháng tán gāngqín, tā xǐhuān liànxí xīngē.)

IV. Homework Assignment: "My Free Time Log"

Students will describe their own or imagined free time routines, applying frequency adverbs and likes/dislikes.

Instructions:

"Write 5-7 sentences for the following diary entries:"

1. My Weekly Hobbies:

Describe 3-4 hobbies or activities you do regularly. For each, mention how often you do it and why you like or dislike it.

Weekly Hobbies: 每週嗜好 (měi zhōu shìhào)

2. Comparing My Family's Hobbies:

Choose two family members or friends. Describe one of their hobbies, how often they do it, and how it compares to your own hobbies or preferences. (e.g., 'My brother often plays video games, but I prefer reading.')

Comparing Hobbies: 比較嗜好 (bǐjiào shìhào)

Remember to use different frequency adverbs and expressions of likes/dislikes. Please write your entries on a separate piece of paper or in your notebook."

請將你的日記寫在一張獨立的紙上或筆記本裡。

(Qǐng jiāng nǐ de rìjì xiě zài yī zhāng dúlì de zhǐ shàng huò bǐjìběn lǐ.)
