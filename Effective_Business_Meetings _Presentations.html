ESL Lesson Plan: Effective Business Meetings & Presentations

Mastering Professional English for Impactful Communication

Target: Business Professionals Level: B1-C1 (CEFR) Time: 60-75 Mins Focus: Meeting & Presentation Skills

I. Lesson Objectives

By the end of this lesson, students will be able to:

  • 📝 Identify key vocabulary and phrases for business meetings and presentations.
  • 🗣️ Practice opening, participating in, and closing meetings effectively.
  • 🎤 Learn strategies for clear and concise presentation delivery.
  • ❓ Develop skills for handling questions and answers professionally.
  • 🤝 Understand basic cross-cultural considerations in business communication.

II. Materials

  • Whiteboard or projector
  • Markers/pens
  • Handouts with meeting agendas, useful phrases, sample presentation slides
  • Optional: Short video clips of business meetings/presentations

III. Lesson Procedure

A. Warm-up: "My Meeting Experience" (10-15 minutes)

Students will share their experiences with business meetings and presentations.

Introduction:

"Hello everyone! Today, we'll focus on 'Effective Business Meetings & Presentations'!"

大家好！今天我們要專注於「高效商務會議與簡報」！

(Dàjiā hǎo! Jīntiān wǒmen yào zhuānzhù yú “gāoxiào shāngwù huìyì yǔ jiǎnbào”!)

Key Vocabulary:

Meeting: 會議 (huìyì)
Presentation: 簡報 (jiǎnbào)
Agenda: 議程 (yìchéng)
Participant: 參與者 (cānyùzhě)
Presenter: 簡報者 (jiǎnbàozhě)
Discussion: 討論 (tǎolùn)
Decision: 決定 (juédìng)
Feedback: 回饋 (huíkuì)

B. Main Activities (40-45 minutes)

Interactive activities to practice meeting participation and presentation skills.

Activity 1: "Meeting Roles & Phrases"

Students will learn and practice key phrases for different roles in a business meeting.

Useful Phrases for Meetings:

Opening & Purpose:
  • Good morning, everyone. Let's start. (大家早安，我們開始吧。)
  • The purpose of this meeting is... (這次會議的目的是...)
Giving Opinion:
  • In my opinion... (在我看來...)
  • I believe that... (我相信...)
Agreeing/Disagreeing:
  • I agree. / I couldn't agree more. (我同意。/ 我非常同意。)
  • I'm afraid I don't agree. (恐怕我不同意。)
Interrupting Politely:
  • Excuse me, may I just add... (不好意思，我能補充一下嗎...)
  • Could I just jump in here? (我能插話一下嗎？)
Summarizing & Closing:
  • To summarize... (總結來說...)
  • Thank you for your participation. (感謝各位的參與。)

Activity: Role-play a short meeting scenario.

Divide students into small groups. Provide a simple meeting agenda (e.g., "Planning a New Project Launch"). Students take on roles (Chairperson, Minute Taker, Participants) and practice using the phrases.

Activity 2: "Presenting with Impact & Q&A"

Students will practice delivering short presentations and handling questions professionally.

Useful Phrases for Presentations & Q&A:

Starting & Structuring:
  • Good morning/afternoon. My name is... (大家早安/午安，我是...)
  • First, I'll talk about... Next, we'll look at... (首先，我會談論...接下來，我們將探討...)
  • As you can see on the slide... (如您在投影片上所見...)
Concluding & Q&A:
  • In conclusion... / To sum up... (總之... / 總結來說...)
  • Thank you for listening. Are there any questions? (感謝聆聽。有任何問題嗎？)
  • That's a good question. (這是個好問題。)
  • I'm happy to answer any questions. (我很樂意回答任何問題。)

Activity: Mini-presentations with Q&A practice.

Students prepare and deliver a very short (1-2 minute) presentation on a simple work-related topic (e.g., "My Department's Role," "A Recent Project," "A New Product Idea"). After each presentation, classmates ask questions, and the presenter practices responding.

C. Wrap-up: "My Meeting Takeaways" (5-10 minutes)

Students will share new phrases or tips they learned for effective meetings and presentations.

Examples:

"I learned to say, 'The purpose of this meeting is...' to start clearly." (我學會了說「這次會議的目的是...」來清楚地開頭。) (Wǒ xuéhuì le shuō “Zhè cì huìyì de mùdì shì...” lái qīngchǔ de kāitóu.)

"Now I know how to ask, 'Are there any questions?' after my presentation." (現在我知道如何在簡報後問「有任何問題嗎？」) (Xiànzài wǒ zhīdào rúhé zài jiǎnbào hòu wèn “Yǒu rènhé wèntí ma?”)

IV. Homework Assignment: "Meeting/Presentation Reflection"

Students will reflect on real-world business communication and prepare for future practice.

Instructions:

"Write 5-7 sentences for the following entries:"

1. Meeting Observation:

Observe a real or online business meeting (it can be in any language). Note down 3-5 English phrases you heard or could have used. Identify one good practice and one area for improvement in the meeting's communication flow.

Meeting Observation: 會議觀察 (huìyì guānchá)

2. Mini-Presentation Outline:

Prepare a short outline (3-5 key points) for a 1-minute presentation on a work-related topic you know well. Think about how you would start, what main points you'd cover, and how you'd conclude.

Presentation Outline: 簡報大綱 (jiǎnbào dàgāng)

"Please write your entries on a separate piece of paper or in your notebook."

請將你的作業寫在一張獨立的紙上或筆記本裡。

(Qǐng jiāng nǐ de zuòyè xiě zài yī zhāng dúlì de zhǐ shàng huò bǐjìběn lǐ.)
