ESL Lesson: Travel Adventures

A Visual Guide to Planning, Experiencing, and Sharing Journeys

A1-B1

CEFR Level

60-75

Minutes

Kids+

Adults

Tenses

Core Skill

Lesson Objectives

  • ✈️Use past, present & future tenses for travel.
  • 🗣️Apply subject pronouns (he, she, they).
  • 🗺️Plan and problem-solve travel scenarios.
  • 📚Expand travel-related vocabulary.

Time Allocation

Main Activities (40-45 mins)

1. Dream Vacation Planner

Groups plan a trip for a chosen person (he, she, we, they), describing past prep, present plans, and future adventures using all three tenses.

Key Vocab:

  • Pack a suitcase (打包行李)
  • Buy a ticket (買票)
  • Book a hotel (預訂飯店)

2. Travel Problem Solvers

Students analyze travel problems (e.g., missed flight, lost passport) and propose solutions, applying correct tenses and pronouns to explain the issue and fix.

Key Vocab:

  • Missed the flight (錯過班機)
  • Lost passport (護照遺失)
  • Got lost (迷路了)

Homework: My Travel Diary

Write 3-5 sentences for each diary entry, using correct tenses and pronouns.
