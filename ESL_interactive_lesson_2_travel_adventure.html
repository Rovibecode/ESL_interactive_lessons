ESL Lesson Plan: Travel Adventures

Planning, Experiencing, and Sharing Journeys

Target: Kids & Adults Level: A1-B1 (CEFR) Time: 60-75 Mins Core Skills: Tenses & Pronouns

I. Lesson Objectives

Building on our previous lesson, students will be able to:

  • ✈️ Accurately use present, past, and future simple tenses to discuss travel plans and experiences.
  • ✈️ Correctly apply subject pronouns (he, she, we, they) when describing people's travel.
  • ✈️ Engage in communicative activities to plan, compare, and problem-solve travel scenarios.
  • ✈️ Expand their vocabulary related to travel and adventures.

II. Materials

  • Whiteboard or projector
  • Markers/pens
  • Worksheet for Activity 2 and Homework
  • Optional: Maps (physical or digital), travel brochures, small prizes

III. Lesson Procedure

A. Warm-up: "Where Did You Go?" (10-15 minutes)

Students will share a past travel experience or imagine one, focusing on simple past tense to kickstart the travel theme.

Introduction:

"Hello adventurers! Today, we're going on a journey to talk about 'Travel Adventures'!"

各位冒險家們好！今天我們要踏上一段旅程，來談談「旅行冒險」！

(Gèwèi màoxiǎnjiāmen hǎo! Jīntiān wǒmen yào tà shàng yīduàn lǚchéng, lái tántán “lǚxíng màoxiǎn”!)

Key Vocabulary:

Travel Adventures: 旅行冒險 (lǚxíng màoxiǎn)
Visited: 參觀過/去過 (cānguānguò/qùguò)
Went sightseeing: 觀光 (guānguāng)
Took photos: 拍照 (pāizhào)
Ate local food: 吃當地美食 (chī dāngdì měishí)

B. Main Activities (40-45 minutes)

Two engaging scenarios requiring students to apply tenses and pronouns in practical travel contexts.

Activity 1: Dream Vacation Planner

Groups plan a dream trip for a chosen person/people (he, she, we, they). They describe past preparations/experiences, present planning activities, and future travel plans, using all three tenses.

Scenario:

"Imagine you are planning a fantastic trip for a friend (he/she), your family (we), or some international visitors (they). Describe what they did to prepare, what they are doing right now to plan, and what they will do on their amazing vacation!"

Key Vocabulary:

Pack a suitcase: 打包行李 (dábāo xínglǐ)

Buy a ticket: 買票 (mǎipiào)

Book a hotel: 預訂飯店 (yùdìng fàndiàn)

Visit a landmark: 參觀地標 (cānguān dìbiāo)

Go hiking: 去爬山 (qù páshān)

Activity 2: Travel Problem Solvers

Students analyze travel-related problem scenarios and propose solutions, using correct tenses and pronouns to describe the problem and its resolution.

Introduction:

"Oh no, travel can have problems! Let's help our friends solve their travel troubles!"

喔不，旅行有時候會遇到問題！讓我們幫朋友解決旅行上的麻煩！

(Ō bù, lǚxíng yǒu shíhòu huì yùdào wèntí! Ràng wǒmen bāng péngyǒu jiějué lǚxíng shàng de máfan!)

Key Vocabulary:

Missed the flight/train: 錯過班機/火車 (cuòguò bānjī/huǒchē)

Lost passport: 護照遺失 (hùzhào yíshī)

Got lost: 迷路了 (mílù le)

Felt sick: 生病了 (shēngbìng le)

Find a solution: 找到解決方案 (zhǎodào jiějué fāng'àn)

C. Wrap-up: "One Travel Sentence" (5-10 minutes)

Each student creates one sentence describing a past, present, or future travel plan/experience, consolidating new vocabulary and grammar.

IV. Homework Assignment: "My Travel Diary"

Students will write short diary entries describing a past trip, their current travel planning, and a future travel dream, applying tenses and pronouns.

Instructions:

"Write 3-5 sentences for each of the following diary entries:"

1. My Last Adventure (Past Tense):

Describe a trip you took in the past.

Last Adventure: 上次的冒險 (shàngcì de màoxiǎn)

2. My Travel Planning Now (Present Tense):

What are you doing to plan a trip right now?

Travel Planning Now: 現在的旅行計劃 (xiànzài de lǚxíng jìhuà)

3. My Dream Trip (Future Tense):

Where will you go for your perfect future trip?

Dream Trip: 夢想之旅 (mèngxiǎng zhī lǚ)

Remember to use 'I' for yourself and try to include 'he,' 'she,' 'we,' or 'they' if you describe someone else in your diary.

"Please write your diary entries on a separate piece of paper or in your notebook."

請將你的日記寫在一張獨立的紙上或筆記本裡。

(Qǐng jiāng nǐ de rìjì xiě zài yī zhāng dúlì de zhǐ shàng huò bǐjìběn lǐ.)
