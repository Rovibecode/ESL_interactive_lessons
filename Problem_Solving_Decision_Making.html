ESL Lesson Plan: Problem-Solving & Decision-Making in Business

Navigating Challenges and Making Informed Choices

Target: Business Professionals Level: B1-C1 (CEFR) Time: 60-75 Mins Focus: Problem Identification, Solution Brainstorming, Decision Justification

I. Lesson Objectives

By the end of this lesson, students will be able to:

  • 🔍 Identify and articulate business problems clearly and concisely.
  • 💡 Brainstorm and propose a range of potential solutions to business challenges.
  • ✅ Discuss and justify decisions based on analysis of pros and cons.
  • ⚠️ Use language to assess risks and potential impacts of decisions.
  • 🗣️ Engage in collaborative discussions to reach consensus or present a chosen solution.

II. Materials

  • Whiteboard or projector
  • Markers/pens
  • Handouts with case studies/scenarios, brainstorming templates, decision matrices
  • Optional: Sticky notes for brainstorming

III. Lesson Procedure

A. Warm-up: "Everyday Challenges" (10-15 minutes)

Students will share simple everyday problems and how they solved them, leading into business contexts.

Introduction:

"Hello everyone! Today, we're going to tackle 'Problem-Solving & Decision-Making in Business'!"

大家好！今天我們要探討「商務問題解決與決策」！

(Dàjiā hǎo! Jīntiān wǒmen yào tàntǎo “shāngwù wèntí jiějué yǔ juécè”!)

Key Vocabulary:

Problem: 問題 (wèntí)
Solution: 解決方案 (jiějué fāng'àn)
Decision: 決定 (juédìng)
Challenge: 挑戰 (tiǎozhàn)
Analyze: 分析 (fēnxī)
Impact: 影響 (yǐngxiǎng)

B. Main Activities (40-45 minutes)

Interactive activities to practice problem identification, solution generation, and decision justification.

Activity 1: "Identifying & Analyzing Business Problems"

Students will practice articulating problems and asking clarifying questions to understand their root causes.

Phrases for Problem Identification & Analysis:

  • The main issue is... (主要問題是...)
  • We're facing a challenge with... (我們正遇到...的挑戰。)
  • What seems to be the root cause? (根本原因似乎是什麼？)
  • Could you provide more details on...? (您能提供更多關於...的細節嗎？)
  • Who is affected by this? (誰受到影響？)

Activity: Problem Description & Clarification.

Provide students with short, vague business problem statements (e.g., "Sales are down," "Customer complaints increased"). In pairs, one student describes the problem, and the other asks clarifying questions using the learned phrases to get a clearer picture.

Activity 2: "Brainstorming Solutions & Justifying Decisions"

Students will practice generating solutions, evaluating pros and cons, and justifying their chosen decisions.

Phrases for Brainstorming & Decision-Making:

  • Let's brainstorm some ideas. (我們來集思廣益一下。)
  • How about we...? / What if we...? (我們不如...？/ 如果我們...？)
  • The pros are... and the cons are... (優點是...而缺點是...)
  • I suggest we choose... because... (我建議我們選擇...因為...)
  • This decision will likely lead to... (這個決定可能會導致...)
  • We need to consider the impact on... (我們需要考慮對...的影響。)

Activity: Solution Pitch & Debate.

Using the clarified problems from Activity 1, students work in groups to brainstorm 2-3 solutions. Each group then presents their best solution, outlining its pros and cons, and justifying their decision. Other groups can ask challenging questions.

C. Wrap-up: "My Problem-Solving Insight" (5-10 minutes)

Students will share one key insight or a practical tip they learned about problem-solving or decision-making.

Examples:

"I learned that it's important to ask many questions to understand the problem fully." (我學到充分理解問題需要問很多問題是很重要的。) (Wǒ xuédào chōngfèn lǐjiě wèntí xūyào wèn hěnduō wèntí shì hěn zhòngyào de.)

"When making a decision, we should always consider the pros and cons carefully." (做決定時，我們應該總是仔細考慮優缺點。) (Zuò juédìng shí, wǒmen yīnggāi zǒngshì zǐxì kǎolǜ yōuquèdiǎn.)

IV. Homework Assignment: "Business Challenge & Solution"

Students will apply problem-solving and decision-making skills to a specific business scenario.

Instructions:

"Write 5-7 sentences for the following entries:"

1. Problem Definition:

Choose a common business problem (e.g., low customer satisfaction, declining sales, team communication issues). Describe the problem clearly, including its symptoms and potential impacts.

Problem Definition: 問題定義 (wèntí dìngyì)

2. Proposed Solution & Justification:

Propose one solution to the problem you defined. Explain why you think this is the best solution, mentioning its advantages (pros) and any potential challenges (cons). Justify your decision.

Proposed Solution: 建議解決方案 (jiànyì jiějué fāng'àn)

"Please write your entries on a separate piece of paper or in your notebook."

請將你的作業寫在一張獨立的紙上或筆記本裡。

(Qǐng jiāng nǐ de zuòyè xiě zài yī zhāng dúlì de zhǐ shàng huò bǐjìběn lǐ.)
