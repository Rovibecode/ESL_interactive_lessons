ESL Curriculum Roadmap

Lessons 1-20: Progressive Skills Development

Lesson Breakdown

Lesson # Topic Core Skills / Focus Key Objectives (Students will be able to...)
1 Daily Life & Routines Present, Past, Future Simple Tenses; Subject Pronouns Describe daily activities using basic tenses & pronouns.
2 Travel Adventures Tenses & Pronouns (applied to travel); Planning & Sharing Discuss travel plans & experiences; plan & problem-solve travel scenarios.
3 Hobbies & Free Time Activities Frequency Adverbs; Likes/Dislikes & Preferences; Comparing Activities Use frequency adverbs; express likes/dislikes; compare hobbies.
4 Amazing Animals & Nature Descriptive Language (Adjectives, Verbs); Habitats; "There is/are"; Comparatives; Frequency Adverbs (integration) Describe animals/nature; use comparatives; integrate frequency adverbs.
5 Food & Culture Food Vocabulary; Descriptive Adjectives (taste/texture); Preferences; Comparatives (food); Frequency Adverbs (eating habits) Describe food with adjectives; express preferences; compare dishes.
6 Describing People & Family Physical/Personality Adjectives; Family Relationships; Possessive Adjectives/Pronouns; "Has/Have" Describe people's appearance & personality; talk about family.
7 Shopping & Money Shopping Vocabulary (clothing, groceries, etc.); Countable/Uncountable Nouns; Quantifiers; Polite Requests Ask prices, make requests; use quantifiers for shopping items.
8 My City & Getting Around City Places; Public Transport; Prepositions of Place; Giving/Asking Directions Describe city locations; give/follow simple directions.
9 Health & Wellness Body Parts; Common Ailments; Feelings; Healthy Habits; Modals for Advice ("should/shouldn't") Talk about health issues; give/receive basic health advice.
10 Future Plans & Aspirations Future Simple ("going to," "will"); Expressing Goals/Dreams; Conditional Sentences (Type 1) Discuss future plans; express goals and aspirations.
11 Daily Life & Routines (Deeper Dive) Idiomatic Daily Vocabulary; Present Continuous; Adverbs of Manner; Present Perfect for related experiences Describe nuanced daily actions; differentiate between habitual & ongoing actions.
12 Technology & Communication Tech Vocabulary; Online Activities; Phrasal Verbs (tech); Imperatives for instructions Discuss various technologies; talk about online activities & communication methods.
13 Education & Learning Paths School Subjects; Educational Institutions; Study Habits; Modals of Necessity; Future Continuous Talk about education system, study habits, and academic goals.
14 Jobs & Careers (Beyond the Basics) Varied Job Titles/Responsibilities; Job Searching; Interview Skills (basic); Passive Voice; Reported Speech (simple) Describe jobs/responsibilities; engage in basic job interview scenarios.
15 Public Holidays & Celebrations Holiday Vocabulary; Cultural Customs; Past Simple/Continuous for narration; Sequence Words Describe holiday traditions & personal experiences; compare celebrations.
16 Arts & Entertainment Genres (music, movies, books); Art Types; Adjectives (-ed/-ing); Nuanced Opinions; Cause and Effect Express opinions on art/entertainment; describe emotional reactions.
17 Environmental Issues & Solutions Environmental Vocabulary (pollution, conservation); Cause and Effect Language; Modals of Suggestion Discuss environmental problems; suggest solutions for sustainability.
18 Problem Solving & Giving Advice Identifying Problems; Varied Advice; Conditional Sentences (Type 2); Phrasal Verbs (problem-solving) Offer advice for hypothetical situations; discuss consequences.
19 Expressing Opinions & Debating Stating Opinions; Agreeing/Disagreeing; Justifications; Connectors (contrasting ideas); Tag Questions State opinions clearly; participate in structured discussions/debates.
20 News & Current Events News Vocabulary; Summarizing; Expressing Reactions; Reported Speech (simple); Certainty/Uncertainty expressions Summarize news articles; discuss current events; express reactions.
