ESL Lesson Plan: Describing People & Family

Understanding and Talking About Ourselves and Others

Target: Kids & Adults Level: A1-B1 (CEFR) Time: 60-75 Mins Focus: Adjectives, Family, Possessives

I. Lesson Objectives

By the end of this lesson, students will be able to:

  • 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Identify and use vocabulary for physical descriptions (e.g., hair color, eye color, height) and personality traits (e.g., kind, shy, outgoing).
  • 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Name and describe various family members and their relationships.
  • 👀 Accurately use adjectives to characterize people's appearance.
  • 😊 Use adjectives to talk about personality and feelings.
  • ❤️ Apply possessive adjectives and pronouns (e.g., my, your, his, hers) to indicate ownership and relationships.
  • 🗣️ Engage in simple conversations about people, asking and answering questions about appearance, personality, and family.
  • ✍️ Write short descriptions of family members or friends, using descriptive language.

II. Materials

  • Whiteboard or projector
  • Markers/pens
  • Pictures/flashcards of diverse people (different ages, appearances) and family scenes
  • Worksheet for Activity 2 and Homework (to be provided by teacher)
  • Optional: Simple props for "Guess Who" (e.g., hats, glasses)

III. Lesson Procedure

A. Warm-up: "Guess Who?" (10-15 minutes)

Students will guess a person (teacher, classmate, famous person) based on simple descriptions to introduce basic vocabulary.

Introduction:

"Hello everyone! Today, we're going to talk about 'Describing People and Family'!"

大家好！今天我們要聊聊「描述人物與家庭」！

(Dàjiā hǎo! Jīntiān wǒmen yào liáoliáo “miáoshù rénwù yǔ jiātíng”!)

Key Vocabulary:

People: 人 (rén)
Family: 家庭 (jiātíng)
Tall: 高的 (gāo de)
Short: 矮的 (ǎi de)
Hair: 頭髮 (tóufà)
Eyes: 眼睛 (yǎnjīng)
Happy: 快樂的 (kuàilè de)
Sad: 悲傷的 (bēishāng de)
Kind: 善良的 (shànliáng de)

B. Main Activities (40-45 minutes)

Interactive activities to practice describing people's appearance, personality, and family relationships.

Activity 1: "Describing Our Friends"

Students use adjectives to describe the physical appearance and personality traits of various people from pictures.

Introduce Descriptive Adjectives (Appearance & Personality):

Appearance:
  • Long hair: 長髮 (chángfà)
  • Short hair: 短髮 (duǎnfà)
  • Curly hair: 捲髮 (juǎnfà)
  • Straight hair: 直髮 (zhífà)
  • Blue eyes: 藍眼睛 (lányǎnjīng)
  • Brown eyes: 棕色眼睛 (zōngsè yǎnjīng)
  • Big nose: 大鼻子 (dà bízi)
  • Small nose: 小鼻子 (xiǎo bízi)
Personality:
  • Shy: 害羞的 (hàixiū de)
  • Outgoing: 外向的 (wàixiàng de)
  • Friendly: 友善的 (yǒushàn de)
  • Serious: 嚴肅的 (yánsù de)
  • Funny: 有趣的 (yǒuqù de)
  • Smart: 聰明的 (cōngmíng de)

Examples:

"She has long, curly hair and blue eyes. She is very friendly." (她有一頭長長的捲髮和藍色的眼睛。她很友善。) (Tā yǒu yì tóu chángcháng de juǎnfà hé lánsè de yǎnjīng. Tā hěn yǒushàn.)

"He is tall and has short, brown hair. He is a funny and outgoing person." (他很高，有一頭棕色的短髮。他是一個有趣又外向的人。) (Tā hěn gāo, yǒu yì tóu zōngsè de duǎnfà. Tā shì yí ge yǒuqù yòu wàixiàng de rén.)

Activity: Show pictures of different people. Students describe them using newly learned adjectives.

Activity 2: "Family Relationships & Possessions"

Students describe family members and use possessive adjectives/pronouns to talk about their relationships and belongings.

Introduce Family Vocabulary & Possessive Adjectives/Pronouns:

Family:
  • Mother / Mom: 媽媽 (māmā)
  • Father / Dad: 爸爸 (bàba)
  • Brother: 哥哥/弟弟 (gēge/dìdi)
  • Sister: 姐姐/妹妹 (jiějie/mèimei)
  • Grandmother / Grandma: 奶奶/外婆 (nǎinai/wàipó)
  • Grandfather / Grandpa: 爺爺/外公 (yéye/wàigōng)
Possessive Adjectives/Pronouns:
  • My: 我的 (wǒ de)
  • Your: 你的 (nǐ de)
  • His: 他的 (tā de)
  • Her: 她的 (tā de)
  • Our: 我們的 (wǒmen de)
  • Their: 他們的 (tāmen de)

Examples:

"This is my sister. Her name is Lily. Her favorite color is pink." (這是我的妹妹。她的名字是Lily。她最喜歡的顏色是粉紅色。) (Zhè shì wǒ de mèimei. Tā de míngzì shì Lily. Tā zuì xǐhuān de yánsè shì fěnhóngsè.)

"This is our dog. Its name is Buddy. Buddy is their pet." (這是我們的狗。牠的名字是Buddy。Buddy是牠們的寵物。) (Zhè shì wǒmen de gǒu. Tā de míngzì shì Buddy. Buddy shì tāmen de chǒngwù.)

Activity: Students share pictures of their family/friends (or imagine them) and describe them using adjectives and possessives.

C. Wrap-up: "My Classmate/Family Member Profile" (5-10 minutes)

Each student creates 1-2 sentences summarizing a classmate or family member, including appearance and a personality trait.

Examples:

"My classmate, Tom, has short black hair and is very smart." (我的同學Tom有一頭烏黑的短髮，而且他很聰明。) (Wǒ de tóngxué Tom yǒu yì tóu wūhēi de duǎnfà, érqiě tā hěn cōngmíng.)

"My mother is beautiful and has long brown hair. She is very kind." (我的媽媽很漂亮，有一頭棕色的長髮。她很善良。) (Wǒ de māmā hěn piàoliang, yǒu yì tóu zōngsè de chángfà. Tā hěn shànliáng.)

IV. Homework Assignment: "My Family Portrait & Description"

Students will describe family members, applying descriptive adjectives and possessive forms.

Instructions:

"Draw a simple picture of your family or a few friends. Then, write 5-7 sentences describing each person you drew. Mention their appearance, one personality trait, and something they like or own. Use possessive adjectives/pronouns."

畫一張你的家庭或幾個朋友的簡單圖畫。然後，寫5-7個句子描述你畫的每個人。提及他們的外貌、一個個性特徵，以及他們喜歡或擁有的東西。使用所有格形容詞/代名詞。

(Huà yī zhāng nǐ de jiātíng huò jǐ gè péngyǒu de jiǎndān túhuà. Ránhòu, xiě wǔ-qī gè jùzi miáoshù nǐ huà de měi ge rén. Tíjí tāmen de wàimào, yí ge gèxìng tèzhēng, yǐjí tāmen xǐhuān huò yǒngyǒu de dōngxi. Shǐyòng suǒyǒugé xíngróngcí/dàimíngcí.)

"Please write your descriptions on a separate piece of paper or in your notebook."

請將你的描述寫在一張獨立的紙上或筆記本裡。

(Qǐng jiāng nǐ de miáoshù xiě zài yī zhāng dúlì de zhǐ shàng huò bǐjìběn lǐ.)
